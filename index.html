The Perfect Partner Is Like the Perfect Pancake

Perkins started as a single pancake house in 1958 and grew into one of America's most recognizable family-dining and bakery brands. Through more than six decades, we've kept evolving our menu and our restaurants — while never sacrificing the quality, value, and warm service that keep guests coming back.

Our franchise opportunity lets qualified operators leverage that legacy into a winning business strategy, backed by a development team committed to helping you succeed in two of the strongest segments in dining.

The most popular meal of the day

All-Day Breakfast, Built-In Bakery

Roughly 70% of Americans want breakfast served any time of day — and at Perkins they can order off our entire menu morning, noon, or night. Breakfast makes up the majority of our sales, and because those ingredients cost less, franchisees often see stronger margins than lunch- and dinner-only concepts.

Then there's the bakery counter. Fresh pies, muffins, and cookies give guests a reason to take Perkins home — a true differentiator that adds a second revenue stream most breakfast concepts simply don't have.

Pies, muffins, cookies, and cakes made to take home turn a single visit into two purchases. Our in-store bakery taps a multi-billion-dollar bakery-café market on top of the breakfast segment — giving Perkins franchisees an edge other concepts can't match.
